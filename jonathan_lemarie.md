# Parmigiana recipe.

![req](/home/dci-student/new_recipes/req.jpg)

 ### ingredient needed :
  - aubergine
  - tomato sauce
  - parmigiano and mozarella


  ### Cooking instruction :

  - slice aubergine and roast them in oven
  - layer them in order following :  aubergine , tomato sauce , parmigiano
